Guide for Learners

Everything you need to become a phonics superstar!

Getting Started

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Visit the App

    Go to alfredang.github.io/phonics-ai

  2. Click Sign Up

    Click "Sign Up" or "Register" to create your account

  3. Enter Your Details

    Enter your email and create a password

  4. Choose a Username

    Pick a fun username that represents you

  5. Start Learning!

    You're ready to begin your phonics journey

Step 2: Start Your Journey

When you first log in, you'll start in Letters Land - the first world where you'll learn individual letter sounds. Don't worry, it's fun and easy!

Pro Tip: Make sure your device volume is on! You'll need to hear the sounds to learn them properly.

How to Complete a Lesson

Every lesson has 4 parts. Complete them all to earn stars!

1

Listen

Watch mouth animations and hear the sounds

2

Practice

Try making the sounds yourself

3

Play

Have fun with games

4

Assess

Test your knowledge

Listen Phase Tips

  • Watch the mouth animation to see how to form the sound
  • Click the speaker button to hear the sound
  • Listen carefully - you can replay as many times as you need
  • Pay attention to example words

Practice Phase Tips

  • Try making the sounds yourself
  • Use the microphone to record your voice
  • Get AI-powered feedback on your pronunciation!
  • Compare your pronunciation with the example
  • Keep practicing until you feel confident
AI Feedback: Our AI analyzes your pronunciation and gives you personalized tips to improve!

Play Phase Tips

  • Play fun games to practice what you learned
  • Try to get the highest score!
  • Games help you remember sounds better
  • You can replay games to improve your score

Assess Phase Tips

  • Take a short quiz to test your knowledge
  • Earn 1, 2, or 3 stars based on your score
  • 3 stars = you're a master!
  • You can retry to earn more stars

The Games

Here are the fun games you'll play:

🍩 Phoneme Pop

Tap the bubbles that have the correct sound. Be quick - they float away!

🔨 Word Builder

Drag letter tiles to build words. Put them in the right order!

🎲 Sound Match

Find matching pairs by listening to sounds. Test your memory!

🏃 Sound Race

Identify sounds as fast as you can. How quick are you?

📝 Sentence Scramble

Put words in the right order to make sentences.

🎤 Karaoke Challenge

Follow along as words light up. Read aloud with the app!

Earning XP and Leveling Up

What is XP?

XP stands for "Experience Points." You earn XP by:

  • Completing lessons
  • Playing games
  • Getting high scores
  • Finishing daily quests
  • Maintaining your streak

Leveling Up

When you earn enough XP, you level up! Higher levels mean:

  • New achievements to unlock
  • Bragging rights with your friends
  • Access to harder challenges
Keep Your Streak: Practice every day to build your streak. The longer your streak, the more bonus XP you earn!

Daily Quests

Every day, you get new quests to complete. These might include:

  • Complete 2 lessons
  • Earn 100 XP
  • Play 3 games
  • Get 3 stars on a lesson

Completing quests gives you bonus XP rewards!

Achievements

Earn special badges by reaching milestones:

Linking to Your Parent's Account

Want your parent to see your awesome progress? You can link your account to theirs! Here's how:

  1. Go to Settings

    Open your dashboard and click on Settings (the gear icon).

  2. Find "Parent Link" Section

    Scroll down to find the Parent Link section.

  3. Generate a Link Code

    Click "Generate Link Code" to get a special 8-character code.

  4. Share the Code

    Tell your parent the code. They'll enter it in their Parent Dashboard.

  5. Approve the Request

    When your parent enters the code, you'll see a request in your Settings. Click the green check to approve!

You're in Control: Only approved parents can see your progress. You can remove a linked parent anytime from your Settings.

Joining Your Teacher's Classroom

If your teacher uses Phonics AI, you can join their classroom so they can see how you're doing!

  1. Get the Code

    Ask your teacher for the classroom join code (6 characters like ABC123).

  2. Go to Settings

    Open your dashboard and click on Settings.

  3. Find "Classroom" Section

    Scroll down to find the Classroom section.

  4. Enter the Code

    Type in the 6-character code and click "Join".

  5. You're In!

    You'll see the classroom name appear. Your teacher can now see your progress!

Good to know: You can join multiple classrooms if you have more than one teacher using the app.

Tips for Success

  1. Practice every day

    Even 10 minutes helps!

  2. Use headphones

    Hear sounds more clearly

  3. Say sounds out loud

    Don't just think them

  4. Watch the mouth animations

    They show you how to form sounds

  5. Don't skip the Listen phase

    It's important!

  6. Replay games

    Practice makes perfect

  7. Ask Phoni for help

    Click the owl in the corner anytime!

Remember: Making mistakes is part of learning! Don't get discouraged. Every expert was once a beginner.

Understanding Phonemes

A phoneme is the smallest unit of sound in a language. English has 44 phonemes!

Categories of Sounds

Category Examples Sample Words
Short Vowels a, e, i, o, u cat, bed, sit, hot, cup
Long Vowels a_e, ee, i_e, o_e, u_e cake, feet, bike, home, cute
Consonants b, c, d, f, g... bat, cat, dog, fan, go
Digraphs sh, ch, th, wh ship, chip, thin, when
Blends bl, cr, st, tr blue, crab, stop, tree
