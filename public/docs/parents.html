Guide for Parents

Supporting your child's phonics journey at home

Welcome, Parents!

Phonics AI is a free, interactive app designed to help children aged 9-15 learn English pronunciation through fun games and structured lessons. This guide will help you understand how the app works and how you can support your child's learning.

🔒

Safe Environment

No ads, no chat, no external links - just educational content

💰

Completely Free

No hidden costs, subscriptions, or in-app purchases

📊

Track Progress

See your child's achievements, XP, and learning milestones

🏠

Learn Anywhere

Works on any device with a web browser

Getting Your Child Started

  1. Create an Account

    Visit the app and help your child sign up with an email address. This saves their progress so they can continue learning on any device.

  2. Set Up the Device

    Make sure sound is enabled and consider using headphones for better focus. Test the audio by clicking any speaker button.

  3. Start in Letters Land

    New learners should begin with the first world to build a strong foundation in letter sounds.

  4. Establish a Routine

    10-15 minutes of daily practice is more effective than longer, infrequent sessions.

How the App Works

The 4-Phase Learning Model

Every lesson follows a proven structure that builds skills progressively:

1

Listen

Your child hears correct pronunciation and sees how to form sounds with mouth animations

2

Practice

They try making the sounds themselves with feedback to guide improvement

3

Play

Fun games reinforce learning and make practice enjoyable

4

Assess

A short quiz tests understanding and awards stars (1-3) based on performance

The 5 Learning Worlds

Your child will progress through 5 themed worlds, each building on the previous one:

1

Letters Land

Individual letter sounds - the building blocks of reading (Ages 9-10)

2

Word City

Blending sounds into simple words like "cat" and "dog" (Ages 10-11)

3

Sentence Street

Reading and understanding simple sentences (Ages 11-12)

4

Paragraph Park

Connected text reading with expression (Ages 12-13)

5

Story Kingdom

Fluent reading of longer passages (Ages 13-15)

Understanding Your Child's Progress

What the Numbers Mean

Metric What It Shows
XP (Experience Points) Overall activity and achievement - higher is better
Level Overall progression - levels up as XP increases
Stars (1-3) Performance on each lesson - 3 stars = 90-100% correct
Streak Consecutive days of practice - encourages daily habits
World Progress Percentage of lessons completed in each world

Star Ratings Explained

  • 3 Stars (90-100%) - Excellent understanding, ready to move forward
  • 2 Stars (70-89%) - Good understanding, may benefit from review
  • 1 Star (50-69%) - Needs more practice on this content
Tip: Encourage your child to retry lessons to earn more stars - this reinforces learning without pressure.

How You Can Help

Daily Support

🕑 Set a Schedule

Pick a consistent time each day for practice - after school, before dinner, or as part of homework time.

🎧 Provide Headphones

Good audio quality helps your child hear sounds clearly and focus without distractions.

👥 Learn Together

Occasionally sit with your child and ask them to teach you the sounds they've learned.

🎉 Celebrate Success

Acknowledge achievements, streaks, and milestones to keep motivation high.

Extending Learning Beyond the App

  • Point out sounds - When reading books together, highlight sounds your child has learned
  • Play sound games - "I spy something that starts with the /k/ sound"
  • Practice writing - Ask your child to write words using the sounds they know
  • Read aloud - Have your child read signs, menus, or labels when out and about
  • Make it fun - Turn practice into games and avoid making it feel like a chore

Screen Time Guidelines

We recommend 10-20 minutes of Phonics AI practice per day. Here's why:

  • Consistent short sessions are more effective than long infrequent ones
  • Daily quests are designed to be completed in about 10-15 minutes
  • Streak rewards encourage regular practice without excessive screen time
  • Quality over quantity - focused practice beats extended sessions
Balance is key: Phonics AI should supplement reading physical books and real-world practice, not replace them.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

My child can't hear the sounds

  1. Check that device volume is turned up
  2. Make sure the browser tab isn't muted
  3. Try using headphones
  4. Refresh the page

The microphone isn't working

  1. Click "Allow" when asked for microphone permission
  2. Check browser settings for microphone access
  3. Close other apps that might be using the microphone
  4. Use Chrome browser for best compatibility

Progress seems lost

  1. Verify your child is logged into the correct account
  2. Check the email address is correct
  3. Try logging out and back in
  4. Refresh the page

For more help, see our Troubleshooting Guide or FAQ.
