The 5 Learning Worlds

Your journey from letters to fluent reading

Overview

Phonics AI is organized into 5 progressive worlds, each designed to build on the skills learned in the previous world. As you complete lessons and earn stars, you'll unlock new worlds and advance toward reading fluency.

Start where you belong: You don't have to begin at World 1. If you already know basic letter sounds, you can start at a higher world that matches your current skill level.

World 1: Letters Land

🌱 The Foundation of Reading

Target Age: 9-10 years | Prerequisite: None

What You'll Learn

  • Individual letter sounds (a-z)
  • Short vowel sounds (/a/, /e/, /i/, /o/, /u/)
  • Common consonant sounds
  • How to form sounds correctly using mouth position

Skills Developed

  • Letter-sound correspondence
  • Phonemic awareness
  • Sound recognition
  • Proper articulation

Sample Lessons

  • The /a/ sound as in "apple" and "cat"
  • The /b/ sound as in "ball" and "bat"
  • Short vowel sounds comparison

Unlocks

Complete 80% of Letters Land lessons to unlock Word City.

World 2: Word City

🏙 Building Your First Words

Target Age: 10-11 years | Prerequisite: Letters Land (80%)

What You'll Learn

  • Blending sounds together to read words
  • CVC (consonant-vowel-consonant) words like "cat", "dog", "run"
  • Word families (-at, -an, -it, -op, etc.)
  • Consonant blends (bl, cr, st, tr)

Skills Developed

  • Sound blending
  • Word decoding
  • Pattern recognition
  • Reading simple words independently

Sample Lessons

  • Blending /c/ + /a/ + /t/ = "cat"
  • The -at family: cat, bat, hat, mat, sat
  • Initial blends: bl-, cr-, st-

Unlocks

Complete 80% of Word City lessons to unlock Sentence Street.

World 3: Sentence Street

🛣 Reading Your First Sentences

Target Age: 11-12 years | Prerequisite: Word City (80%)

What You'll Learn

  • Reading simple sentences fluently
  • Common sight words (the, is, are, was, etc.)
  • Basic punctuation and pausing
  • Long vowel sounds and patterns

Skills Developed

  • Sentence fluency
  • Sight word recognition
  • Phrasing and expression
  • Reading comprehension basics

Sample Lessons

  • "The cat sat on the mat."
  • Sight words: the, is, are, was, were
  • Long /a/ sound: cake, make, lake

Unlocks

Complete 80% of Sentence Street lessons to unlock Paragraph Park.

World 4: Paragraph Park

🌳 Connected Text Reading

Target Age: 12-13 years | Prerequisite: Sentence Street (80%)

What You'll Learn

  • Reading multiple sentences together
  • Prosody (rhythm and expression)
  • Advanced phoneme patterns (digraphs, diphthongs)
  • R-controlled vowels (ar, er, ir, or, ur)

Skills Developed

  • Reading fluency
  • Expression and intonation
  • Comprehension of longer texts
  • Self-correction strategies

Sample Lessons

  • Reading 3-4 sentence passages
  • Digraphs: sh, ch, th, wh
  • Expression practice: questions vs. statements

Unlocks

Complete 80% of Paragraph Park lessons to unlock Story Kingdom.

World 5: Story Kingdom

🏰 Becoming a Fluent Reader

Target Age: 13-15 years | Prerequisite: Paragraph Park (80%)

What You'll Learn

  • Reading complete stories and passages
  • Advanced vocabulary and complex words
  • Silent letter patterns
  • Multisyllable word decoding

Skills Developed

  • Fluent, expressive reading
  • Deep comprehension
  • Independent reading strategies
  • Confident oral reading

Sample Lessons

  • Short story readings with comprehension
  • Silent letters: knight, write, comb
  • Breaking down multisyllable words

Completion Reward

Complete Story Kingdom to earn the Master Reader achievement!

World Progression Chart

World Focus Target Age Unlock Requirement
1. Letters Land Individual sounds 9-10 None (starting world)
2. Word City Simple words 10-11 80% of World 1
3. Sentence Street Simple sentences 11-12 80% of World 2
4. Paragraph Park Connected text 12-13 80% of World 3
5. Story Kingdom Fluent reading 13-15 80% of World 4

Tips for Success

⏱ Go at Your Own Pace

There's no rush! Take the time you need to master each world before moving on.

⭐ Aim for 3 Stars

Try to get 3 stars on lessons before progressing. This ensures you've mastered the content.

🔁 Replay When Needed

You can always go back and replay lessons from earlier worlds to reinforce skills.

🔥 Keep Your Streak

Daily practice builds skills faster than occasional long sessions.
