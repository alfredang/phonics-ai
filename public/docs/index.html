Welcome to Phonics AI

Your interactive guide to mastering English pronunciation through games and fun!

What is Phonics AI?

Phonics AI is a free, interactive web application designed to help students aged 9-15 learn English pronunciation through engaging games, structured lessons, and AI-powered feedback.

🎮

Fun Games

Learn through interactive games like Phoneme Pop, Word Builder, and Sound Match.

🎯

44 Phonemes

Master all 44 English sounds with visual mouth guides and audio examples.

🏆

Earn Rewards

Collect XP, unlock achievements, and complete daily quests as you learn.

🗣

Speech Practice

Listen to correct pronunciations and practice speaking with voice recording.

Quick Start

Get started with Phonics AI in just a few steps:

  1. Visit the App

    Go to alfredang.github.io/phonics-ai

  2. Create an Account

    Sign up with your email to save your progress

  3. Start in Letters Land

    Begin with the first world to learn basic letter sounds

  4. Complete Lessons

    Work through each lesson's 4 phases: Listen, Practice, Play, Assess

  5. Earn XP & Level Up

    Collect rewards and unlock new worlds as you progress

Tip: Practice every day to build your streak and earn bonus XP!

How It Works

The 4-Phase Learning Approach

Every lesson follows a proven pedagogical framework:

1

Listen

Hear sounds with visual mouth guides

2

Practice

Try the sounds with feedback

3

Play

Reinforce through games

4

Assess

Test and earn stars

5 Learning Worlds

Progress through themed worlds, each focusing on different phonics skills:

1

Letters Land

Learn individual letter sounds (Ages 9-10)

2

Word City

Blend sounds into simple words (Ages 10-11)

3

Sentence Street

Read simple sentences fluently (Ages 11-12)

4

Paragraph Park

Master connected text reading (Ages 12-13)

5

Story Kingdom

Become a fluent reader (Ages 13-15)
